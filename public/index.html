Introducing Mana Guide

Mana Guide is an upcoming mobile database app for Magic: The Gathering.

All Sets

Contains all Sets from Limited Edition Alpha to the latest set.

Advanced Card Search

Find the specific cards you need with advanced filters.

Card Pricing

View card pricing data from TCGPlayer.com

Deck Builder

Build decks, and also view decks built by other people.

Collection Builder

Manage your card collection via the Collection Builder.

Card Rating

Rate cards and view the top rated cards.

iOS App

Native iOS app coming soon.

Android App

Native Android app coming soon.

Built by Magic Players for Magic Players

Mana Guide started out as a need for a player to know and find up-to-date information on his cards.
